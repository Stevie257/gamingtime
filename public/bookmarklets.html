Usage


Just click and drag the bookmarklet (blue text) onto your bookmarks bar, and click it to use it









  Color
  


Creates a color menu on the left side of the screen. Click and drag the colors onto your webpage to color the elements!












  Trip


Randomly tilts everything on the screen











  Rainbow


Makes everything a random color


  








  Strobe


Rainbow, but happenes every milisecond



  








  Earthquake


Trip, but happenes every milisecond



  







  3D Webpage


Makes the webpage 3D! Comes with a menu to adjust the settings



    







  Ship


Spawns a ship on your screen that can blow up elements! Use the arrow keys to fly and space to shoot











  Flashlight


A neat flashlight with realistic light effects











  Edit , 
  UnEdit


Changes all the text on the screen to be editable, as if it was a document. Unedit just disables this












  Flip


Flips the webpage












  Glow


Makes everything on the screen glow rainbow












  Minesweeper


Adds minesweeper to your screen!





  








  Star Wars


Rotates your webpage and slowly scrolls like Star Wars. Comes iwth music!





  







  Embedder


Adds a custom webpage do the bottom of the current one





  







  Uglyify


Randomly changes everything on the screen's color and size. Also changes font to Comic Sans



  







  Insanity


Adds a tough little game to your screen!



  







  Kill


Deletes whatever element you click after activating



  







  Xray


A very powerful tool similar to the Inspect mode for Chrome




  







  Clicker


An autoclicker! Will rapidly click whatever element you click on after activation



  







  FPS Readout


Displays the current frames per second of the webpage




  







  Piano


Adds a playable piano to your webpage
